# UPSC Prelims Preparation
## Strategy & Resources for Preliminary Examination

The Preliminary Examination is the first stage of UPSC Civil Services, consisting of two objective-type papers. It acts as a screening test for the Mains examination.

---

## üìã Exam Structure

### Paper I - General Studies
- **Marks**: 200
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Questions**: 100 (2 marks each)
- **Negative Marking**: 1/3 (0.66 marks)
- **Qualifying**: Marks count for final merit

### Paper II - CSAT (Civil Services Aptitude Test)
- **Marks**: 200
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Questions**: 80 (2.5 marks each)
- **Negative Marking**: 1/3 (0.83 marks)
- **Qualifying**: 33% minimum (66 marks)

## üéØ Preparation Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (6-9 months before)

**NCERT Reading (Class 6-12)**
- History: 6-12
- Geography: 6-12
- Polity: 9-12
- Economics: 9-12
- Science: 6-10

**Benefits:**
- Builds strong foundation
- Clears concepts
- UPSC loves NCERT questions
- Easy language

**Time Required:** 2-3 months

**Subject-Wise Books:**

- Budget Documents

**Environment**
- Environment - Shankar IAS

**Science & Technology**
- Science & Technology - Ravi Agrahari

**Time Required:** 3-4 months

---

### Phase 3: Current Affairs (Ongoing)

**Daily:**
- The Hindu/Indian Express (1 hour)
- Make notes of important topics

**Weekly:**
- Yojana, Kurukshetra magazines
- Science Reporter
- Down to Earth

**Monthly:**
- Vision IAS Monthly Magazine
- Chronicle Monthly

**Coverage:**
- National & International Events
- Government Schemes
- Reports & Indices
- Science & Technology
- Awards & Honors
- Sports
- Personalities

---

### Phase 4: Practice & Revision (3-4 months before)

**Previous Year Questions (PYQs)**
- Last 10 years (2014-2023)
- Topic-wise analysis
- Identify patterns
- Focus on repeated topics

**Test Series**
- Join a good test series
- Give at least 20-25 tests
- Analyze each test thoroughly
- Work on weak areas

**Revision**
- Multiple revisions (3-4 times minimum)
- Make concise notes
- Use mind maps
- Focus on facts, data, names

## üéì CSAT Preparation Tips

### Key Points:
- CSAT is qualifying (33% minimum)
- Focus on accuracy, not speed initially
- Practice regularly
- Cover all question types

### Strategy:
1. **Comprehension** (Most Important)
   - Read newspapers/editorials
   - Practice passages daily
   - Improve reading speed

2. **Basic Math**
   - Revise class 10 mathematics
   - Practice mental calculations
   - Learn shortcuts

3. **Logical Reasoning**
   - Understand question types
   - Practice different patterns
   - Learn to eliminate options

4. **Data Interpretation**
   - Practice with different formats
   - Improve calculation speed
   - Learn to approximate

## üí° Smart Tips & Tricks

### For Paper I

**Reading Strategy:**
- Read question first, then options
- Eliminate obviously wrong answers
- Don't spend > 1 minute per question
- Mark difficult questions, return later

**Guessing Strategy:**
- Guess only if you can eliminate 2 options
- Trust your first instinct
- Don't change answers unless sure

**Time Management:**
- Divide 120 minutes wisely
- First pass: Known questions (60-70 mins)
- Second pass: Doubtful questions (30-40 mins)
- Third pass: Educated guesses (10-20 mins)

**Negative Marking Management:**
- Be cautious
- Attempt 75-85 questions with confidence
- Quality > Quantity
- 90-95 correct answers = Safe score

---

### For Paper II

**Strategy:**
- Attempt comprehension first
- Cover easy math/reasoning
- Leave difficult calculations
- Time management is crucial

**Target:**
- Aim for 70-75 marks (35-37 questions correct)
- Don't waste time on tough questions
- Focus on clearing cutoff
