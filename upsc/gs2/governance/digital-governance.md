## üîí Privacy & Data Protection

:::details **Right to Privacy - Puttaswamy Judgment (2017)**
- **Supreme Court**: Privacy as fundamental right
- **Article 21**: Right to life includes privacy
- **Intrinsic Right**: Inalienable natural right
- **Limitations**: Reasonable restrictions possible
- **Test**: Legality, legitimate aim, proportionality
- **Impact**: On Aadhaar, surveillance, data protection
:::

:::details **Aadhaar Integration - Constitutional Position**
- **Upheld (2018)**: With conditions by 4:1 majority
- **Legitimate Aim**: Welfare delivery, preventing leakage
- **Restrictions**: 
  - No mandatory linking with bank accounts
  - No mandatory linking with mobile phones
  - Section 57 struck down (private entities can't demand)
- **Safeguards**: Data protection regulations required
- **Concerns**: Surveillance, profiling, data security
:::

:::details **Data Protection Framework**
- **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**: Enacted
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Consent for data processing
  - Right to access and correction
  - Right to erasure and data portability
  - Data localization requirements
  - Penalties for violations
- **Data Protection Board**: Regulatory body
- **Exemptions**: Government for security and sovereignty
:::

:::details **Surveillance & Security Concerns**
- **Central Monitoring System**: Lawful interception
- **Pegasus Controversy**: Spyware allegations
- **Facial Recognition**: NCRB database concerns
- **CCTNS**: Crime tracking network
- **Social Media Monitoring**: Government oversight
- **Balance Needed**: Security vs privacy rights
:::

## üì± Digital Divide Challenges

:::details **Access Inequality**
- **Internet Penetration**: Only 38% of population
- **Rural-Urban Gap**: 25% rural vs 61% urban
- **Gender Digital Divide**: 31% women vs 52% men
- **Age Factor**: Elderly excluded from digital services
- **Disability**: Accessibility issues for persons with disabilities
- **Affordability**: Data and device costs barrier
:::

:::details **Infrastructure Gaps**
- **Broadband**: Limited in rural areas
- **Electricity**: Power supply issues
- **Devices**: Smartphone penetration low
- **Digital Literacy**: Skills gap significant
- **Language Barrier**: English-dominated interfaces
- **Maintenance**: Poor service support in remote areas
:::

:::details **Exclusion Risks**
- **Essential Services**: Only online creates exclusion
- **Welfare Schemes**: Digital-only enrollment problems
- **Financial Services**: Banking without internet
- **Education**: Online learning access gap
- **Healthcare**: Telemedicine infrastructure needs
- **Employment**: Digital job applications only
:::
