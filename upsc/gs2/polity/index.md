## üîë Quick Revision

:::details **Important Articles**
- **Article 1**: Name and territory
- **Article 12-35**: Fundamental Rights
- **Article 32**: Constitutional remedies
- **Article 36-51**: DPSP
- **Article 51A**: Fundamental Duties
- **Article 79-122**: Parliament
- **Article 124-147**: Supreme Court
- **Article 243**: Panchayati Raj
- **Article 324**: Election Commission
- **Article 352-360**: Emergencies
:::

:::details **Key Amendments**
- **42nd (1976)**: Mini Constitution
- **44th (1978)**: Restored balance
- **73rd (1992)**: Panchayati Raj
- **74th (1992)**: Municipalities
- **86th (2002)**: Right to Education
- **99th (2014)**: NJAC (struck down)
- **101st (2016)**: GST
- **103rd (2019)**: EWS reservation
:::

:::details **Landmark Cases**
- **Kesavananda Bharati (1973)**: Basic structure
- **Maneka Gandhi (1978)**: Article 21 expanded
- **S.R. Bommai (1994)**: Article 356 safeguards
- **Vishaka (1997)**: Sexual harassment
- **Second Judges (1993)**: Collegium system
- **Puttaswamy (2017)**: Privacy right
:::
