## üïâÔ∏è Bhakti Movement

### North India

:::details **Ramanuja (1017-1137)**
- **Born**: Tamil Nadu originally
- **Philosophy**: Vishishtadvaita propounded
- **Qualified**: Non-dualism doctrine
- **Ramanand**: Disciple famous
- **Vaishnavism**: Sri Sampradaya founder
:::

:::details **Madhvacharya (1238-1317)**
- **Born**: Karnataka region
- **Philosophy**: Dvaita propounded
- **Dualism**: Doctrine of reality
- **Vishnu**: Supreme god devotion
- **Dvaita**: School established firmly
:::

:::details **Ramananda (1400-1470)**
- **Born**: Prayag (Allahabad)
- **Guru**: Ramanuja disciple
- **Disciples**: 12 main followers
- **Hindi**: Language used vernacular
- **Caste**: Equality emphasized
:::

:::details **Ramananda's Disciples**
- **Kabir**: Weaver sant poet
- **Ravidas**: Cobbler saint
- **Sen**: Barber caste saint
- **Dhanna**: Jat caste saint
- **Pipa**: Rajput prince saint
:::

### Sant Tradition

:::details **Kabir (1440-1518)**
- **Born**: Varanasi city
- **Parents**: Muslim weaver family
- **Guru**: Ramananda disciple
- **Philosophy**: Hindu-Muslim synthesis
- **Dohas**: Two-line couplets composed
- **Nirguna**: Formless god concept
- **Equality**: All religions equal
- **Criticism**: Rituals, caste opposed
- **Bijak**: Main work composition
:::

:::details **Guru Nanak (1469-1539)**
- **Born**: Talwandi (Pakistan)
- **Founder**: Sikhism religion
- **Guru**: Ramananda disciple
- **Philosophy**: Monotheism emphasized
- **Nirguna**: Formless god worship
- **Equality**: Caste discrimination opposed
- **Langar**: Community kitchen started
- **Guru**: Granth Sahib compiled
- **Adi**: Granth scripture final
:::

:::details **Ravidas (1377-1527)**
- **Born**: Varanasi city
- **Caste**: Cobbler family
- **Guru**: Ramananda disciple
- **Philosophy**: Bhakti devotion
- **Equality**: Social equality advocated
- **Poetry**: Devotional poems composed
:::

### East India

:::details **Chaitanya Mahaprabhu (1486-1534)**
- **Born**: Navadvipa Bengal
- **Philosophy**: Gaudiya Vaishnavism
- **Krishna**: Radha worship emphasized
- **Sankirtan**: Group singing started
- **Bhakti**: Emotional devotion
- **ISKCON**: Movement influenced later
- **Bengal**: Vaishnavism spread
:::

:::details **Sankardev (1449-1568)**
- **Born**: Assam region
- **Neo-Vaishnavism**: Assam movement
- **Eka**: Sarana Nam Dharma
- **Monasteries**: Satras established
- **Dance**: Bihu, Sattriya promoted
- **Culture**: Assamese culture enriched
:::

### West India

:::details **Narsinh Mehta (1414-1481)**
- **Born**: Gujarat region
- **Poetry**: Bhakti poems composed
- **Vaishnavism**: Krishna devotion
- **Gujarati**: Language used extensively
- **Mahatma**: Gandhi influenced
:::

### South India

:::details **Meera Bai (1498-1547)**
- **Born**: Rajasthan Merta
- **Princess**: Rajput royal family
- **Krishna**: Devotion to god
- **Poetry**: Devotional songs composed
- **Rajasthan**: Bhakti popularized
- **Vrindavan**: Lived there later
:::

:::details **Tulsidas (1532-1623)**
- **Born**: Varanasi city
- **Epic**: Ramcharitmanas composed
- **Language**: Awadhi dialect used
- **Rama**: Devotion to god
- **Hindi**: Literature enriched
:::

## üåô Sufi Movement

### Major Sufi Orders

:::details **Chishti Order**
- **Founder**: Moinuddin Chishti
- **Ajmer**: Dargah located
- **1141**: Chishti born
- **Sama**: Music in worship
- **Love**: Divine love emphasized
- **Service**: Humanity service important
:::

:::details **Moinuddin Chishti (1141-1236)**
- **Born**: Sistan region
- **Ajmer**: Settled permanently
- **Dargah**: Famous pilgrimage site
- **Ajmer**: Sharif shrine
- **Garib**: Nawaz helper poor
- **Universal**: Love message preached
:::

:::details **Nizamuddin Auliya (1238-1325)**
- **Born**: Badayun Uttar Pradesh
- **Delhi**: Major center established
- **Dargah**: Nizamuddin shrine
- **Disciples**: Many famous saints
- **Chishti**: Order promoted extensively
- **Sama**: Music, dance allowed
:::

### Other Orders

:::details **Suhrawardi Order**
- **Founder**: Bahauddin Zakariya
- **Multan**: Center established
- **Orthodox**: More conservative
- **Sharia**: Emphasized strictly
- **Sama**: Music discouraged generally
:::

:::details **Qadiri Order**
- **Founder**: Abdul Qadir Gilani
- **Baghdad**: Origin center
- **India**: Spread later period
- **Mystical**: Philosophy emphasized
:::

### Famous Sufi Saints

:::details **Baba Farid (1173-1266)**
- **Born**: Punjab region
- **Pakpattan**: Dargah located
- **Punjabi**: Language used
- **Guru**: Nanak influenced
- **Poetry**: Punjabi verses composed
:::

:::details **Salim Chishti (1478-1572)**
- **Born**: Fatehpur Sikri
- **Akbar**: Emperor devotee
- **Son**: Birth predicted
- **Fatehpur**: Sikri built
- **Dargah**: Famous shrine
:::
