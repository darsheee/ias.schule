- **War Indemnity**: 3 crore rupees

:::details **Fourth Anglo-Mysore War (1799)**

## üìú Annexation Policies

:::details **Subsidiary Alliance (Wellesley)**
- **Introduced**: 1798 Wellesley
- **Conditions**: British troops stationed
- **No Foreign**: Relations allowed
- **British Resident**: Court posted
- **Internal Autonomy**: Nominal only
- **First**: Hyderabad 1798
:::

:::details **States Under Subsidiary Alliance**
- **Hyderabad**: 1798 first
- **Mysore**: 1799 after war
- **Tanjore**: 1799 annexed
- **Awadh**: 1801 accepted
- **Peshwa**: 1802 Bassein
- **Bhonsle**: 1803 Nagpur
- **Scindia**: 1803 Gwalior
:::

:::details **Doctrine of Lapse (Dalhousie)**
- **Introduced**: 1848 Dalhousie
- **No Heir**: State annexed
- **Adoption**: Not recognized
- **Satara**: 1848 first
- **Jaipur**: 1849 annexed
- **Sambhalpur**: 1849 annexed
- **Baghat**: 1850 annexed
- **Udaipur**: 1852 annexed
- **Jhansi**: 1853 annexed
- **Nagpur**: 1854 annexed
:::

:::details **Other Annexations**
- **Awadh**: 1856 misgovernance
- **Punjab**: 1849 war
- **Lower Burma**: 1852 war
- **Berar**: 1853 treaty
