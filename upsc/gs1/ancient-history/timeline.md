::: tip Quick Reference
This timeline covers Ancient India from the Late Vedic Period through the Gupta Golden Age. Key periods include the Mahajanapadas (600 BCE), Mauryan Empire (321-184 BCE), Indo-Greek/Kushan Period (200 BCE-300 CE), and Gupta Empire (240-550 CE).
:::
