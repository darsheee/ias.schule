:::details **Harappa (Punjab, Pakistan)**

- **Size**: Large urban settlement
- **Significance**: First discovered site

:::details **Mohenjo-daro (Sindh, Pakistan)**

- **Location**: Indus river bank
- **Great Bath**: 12m x 7m
- **Assembly Hall**: Found here
- **Grid Pattern**: Well-planned streets
- **Largest Site**: Most excavated
:::

:::details **Dholavira (Gujarat)**
- **Location**: Khadir island, Kutch
- **Water Management**: Advanced system
- **Reservoirs**: 16 found here
- **Signboard**: Ten Indus signs
- **Stadiums**: Large open spaces
- **Three Parts**: Citadel, middle, lower
:::

:::details **Lothal (Gujarat)**
- **Location**: Bhogavo river, Sabarmati
- **Dockyard**: Evidence of maritime
- **Bead-making**: Factory found here
- **Rice Husk**: Evidence found
- **Fire Altars**: Ritual structures
- **Trade Center**: Important port
:::

:::details **Kalibangan (Rajasthan)**
- **Location**: Ghaggar river (Saraswati)
- **Fire Altars**: Earliest evidence
- **Ploughed Field**: Evidence found
- **Pre-Harappan**: Settlement below Harappan
- **Earthquake**: Evidence of destruction
:::

:::details **Rakhigarhi (Haryana)**
- **Size**: Largest Harappan site
- **Area**: 350 hectares approx
- **Location**: Ghaggar river basin
- **Cemetery**: Five discovered here
- **Granary**: Found at site
:::

:::details **Banawali (Haryana)**
- **Location**: Ghaggar river basin
- **Barley**: Remains found here
- **Toys**: Terracotta models discovered
- **Beads**: Manufacturing evidence found

:::details **Chanhudaro (Sindh, Pakistan)**
