# Ethics: Essence, Determinants & Consequences
## Understanding Ethical Foundations

---

## üéØ What is Ethics?

:::details **Definition**
- **Ethics**: Moral principles study
- **Right**: Wrong distinction made
- **Conduct**: Rules governing behavior
- **Philosophy**: Branch dealing morality
- **Greek**: Ethos means character
:::

:::details **Ethics vs Morality**
- **Ethics**: Codified, systematic principles
- **Morality**: Personal beliefs values
- **Ethics**: External standards set
- **Morality**: Internal compass guides
- **Ethics**: Professional codes included
- **Morality**: Individual conscience based
:::

:::details **Ethics vs Law**
- **Law**: Enforceable by state
- **Ethics**: Voluntary compliance based
- **Law**: Minimum standard required
- **Ethics**: Higher standard expected
- **Punishment**: Law has penalties
- **Guilt**: Ethics has remorse
:::

## üí´ Consequences of Ethics

### Individual Level

:::details **Personal Benefits**
- **Peace**: Of mind achieved
- **Self-respect**: Maintained always intact
- **Integrity**: Character strengthened significantly
- **Trust**: Others trust earned
- **Happiness**: Long-term contentment gained
:::

:::details **Personal Costs**
- **Short-term**: Loss possible sometimes
- **Pressure**: From unethical peers
- **Sacrifice**: Immediate gains foregone
- **Difficult**: Choices faced regularly
:::

### Social Level

:::details **Social Benefits**
- **Trust**: Society functions better
- **Cooperation**: Increased among people
- **Harmony**: Peace maintained effectively
- **Development**: Sustainable progress ensured
- **Justice**: Social order established
:::

:::details **Social Costs**
- **Corruption**: Trust erodes significantly
- **Injustice**: Inequality increases rapidly
- **Conflict**: Social tensions rise
- **Breakdown**: Of institutions occurs
:::

### Organizational Level

:::details **Organizational Benefits**
- **Reputation**: Enhanced brand value
- **Loyalty**: Employee retention improved
- **Performance**: Better results achieved
- **Sustainability**: Long-term success ensured
:::

:::details **Organizational Costs**
- **Scandals**: Reputation damaged severely
- **Penalties**: Legal consequences faced
- **Turnover**: Employees leave quickly
- **Failure**: Business collapse possible
:::
