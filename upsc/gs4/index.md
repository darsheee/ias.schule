## ðŸŽ¯ Study Approach

### For Theory Portion
1. Understand concepts clearly
2. Make notes with examples
3. Connect with real-life situations
4. Read works of moral thinkers
5. Follow current ethical issues

### For Case Studies
1. Practice regularly
2. Identify all stakeholders
3. List all ethical issues
4. Consider multiple perspectives
5. Suggest balanced solutions
6. Write in structured format

## ðŸ“ Answer Writing Strategy

### Theory Questions (10-15 marks)
- **Define** the concept
- **Explain** dimensions
- **Give examples** from real life
- **Conclude** with relevance

### Case Studies (20-25 marks)
- **Facts** - Summarize the case
- **Stakeholders** - Identify all parties
- **Ethical Issues** - List conflicts/dilemmas
- **Analysis** - Examine from multiple angles
- **Course of Action** - Suggest solutions
- **Conclusion** - Final recommendation
