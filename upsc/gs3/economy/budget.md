## üìä Budget Components

### Revenue Budget

:::details **Revenue Receipts**
- **Tax**: Revenue major source
- **Non-tax**: Revenue other sources
- **Interest**: Receipts on loans
- **Dividends**: From PSUs received
- **Fees**: Fines collected regularly
:::

:::details **Tax Revenue**
- **Direct**: Tax on income
- **Indirect**: Tax on goods
- **Income**: Tax major contributor
- **GST**: Biggest indirect tax
- **Corporation**: Tax from companies
:::

:::details **Non-Tax Revenue**
- **Interest**: On loans given
- **Dividends**: From PSUs profits
- **Fees**: Government services charged
- **Fines**: Penalties collected regularly
- **Grants**: From foreign sources
:::

:::details **Revenue Expenditure**
- **Salary**: Government employees paid
- **Pensions**: Retired employees given
- **Interest**: On public debt
- **Subsidies**: Various schemes provided
- **Grants**: To states given
- **Defense**: Non-capital expenditure included
:::

---

### Capital Budget

:::details **Capital Receipts**
- **Borrowings**: Internal, external loans
- **Disinvestment**: PSU stakes sold
- **Recovery**: Of loans given
- **Market**: Borrowings major source
- **Small**: Savings schemes included
:::

:::details **Capital Expenditure**
- **Infrastructure**: Roads, bridges built
- **Machinery**: Equipment purchased regularly
- **Buildings**: Constructed for offices
- **Loans**: To states, PSUs
- **Defense**: Capital purchases included
:::

## üí∞ Budget Deficits

:::details **Revenue Deficit**
- **Formula**: Revenue expenditure minus
- **Indicates**: Dissaving of government
- **Meet**: Through borrowings only
- **Current**: Expenditure exceeds receipts
:::

:::details **Fiscal Deficit**
- **Formula**: Total expenditure minus
- **Borrowing**: Requirement of government
- **Target**: 3% of GDP
- **FRBM**: Act mandates limit
- **Most**: Important deficit indicator
:::

:::details **Primary Deficit**
- **Formula**: Fiscal deficit minus
- **Interest**: Payments excluded completely
- **Pure**: Borrowing requirement shown
- **Better**: Indicator than fiscal
:::

:::details **Effective Revenue Deficit**
- **Formula**: Revenue deficit minus
- **Capital**: Expenditure grants excluded
- **13th**: Finance Commission introduced
- **Quality**: Of expenditure measured
:::

## üìà FRBM Act

:::details **Fiscal Responsibility Act (2003)**
- **Objective**: Fiscal discipline maintain
- **Targets**: Deficit reduction set
- **Fiscal**: Deficit 3% GDP
- **Revenue**: Deficit zero target
- **Annual**: Targets specified clearly
:::

:::details **FRBM Targets**
- **Fiscal**: Deficit 3% GDP
- **Revenue**: Deficit eliminate completely
- **Debt**: 40% GDP center
- **State**: Debt 20% GDP
:::

:::details **FRBM Amendments**
- **2018**: NK Singh Committee
- **Debt**: To GDP ratio
- **Escape**: Clause introduced flexibility
- **Target**: 3% by 2021
:::
